About Me

HI am originally from the serene village of Gyalposhing in Namgaycholling Geog, nestled within the district of Samtse Dzongkhag. Presently, I am pursuing my studies at Sherubtse College, where I am driven to expand my knowledge and explore new perspectives. Learning holds a deep place in my heart, and I find immense pleasure in engaging in meaningful conversations that allow me to connect with people on various topics. Playing badminton is one of my cherished pastimes; it not only keeps me active but also brings a sense of joy and challenge.

Beyond academics and hobbies, my greatest source of encouragement comes from my family. Their kindness and unwavering support empower me to pursue my goals with confidence and resilience. I am grateful for the values they instilled in me, and they continually inspire me to strive for excellence while remaining grounded.
